# Privacy Policy

**YOLOcide**
Last updated: April 29, 2025

---

## Overview

YOLOcide is a decision-making roulette wheel app. Your privacy matters, so we kept things simple: the app collects as little data as possible and never sells, shares, or monetizes it.

---

## Data stored on your device

All core app data lives locally on your device using Apple's standard storage (UserDefaults). This includes:

- Your wheel options
- Your spin history
- App preferences (language, appearance, haptics)

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly sign in (see below). It is not sent to any server, and we have no access to it.

---

## Account & authentication (optional)

Signing in is entirely optional. If you choose to sign in with Apple or Google, we store the following on our servers:

| Field              | Purpose                                             |
| ------------------ | --------------------------------------------------- |
| Provider user ID   | Identify your account                               |
| Email address      | Account recovery (when provided by Apple or Google) |
| Display name       | Personalisation (when provided by Apple or Google)  |
| Sign-in timestamps | Security and abuse prevention                       |

We do not store passwords. Authentication is handled entirely by Apple or Google — we only receive a verified token.

---

## Data we do NOT collect

- We do not collect analytics or usage data
- We do not use advertising SDKs or tracking pixels
- We do not share data with third parties
- We do not sell data — ever
- We do not use cookies

---

## Third-party services

If you sign in, your authentication request passes through:

- **Apple Sign In** — governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)
- **Google Sign In** — governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

No other third-party services are integrated.

---

## Data retention & deletion

You can delete your spin history at any time from within the app. If you have an account and wish to delete it entirely, contact us at the email below and we will remove your data from our servers within 30 days.

---

## Children's privacy

YOLOcide does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

---

## Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
