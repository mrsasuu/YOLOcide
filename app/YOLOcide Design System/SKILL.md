If the user invokes this skill without any other guidance, ask them what they want to build or design, ask some questions, and act as an expert designer who outputs HTML artifacts _or_ production code, depending on the need.

Core YOLOcide cues to never forget:
- iOS-native Liquid Glass × Radix aesthetic. Soft pastel wheel slices separated by **pure white hairlines** are the single most identifiable motif — never omit the divider.
- Six core pastels (lavender, sky, mint, peach, rose, violet) — opaque in light, ~70% α in dark on `#3a3d4a`.
- One accent: `#6c5ce7` — primary CTA only. No gradients, no secondary accents.
- Voice: playful, fatalistic, second-person, short. "Spin my fate" not "Spin". "Fate has spoken."
- Spring motion everywhere. Press = `scale(0.97)`. No flat fades for state transitions.
- Option rows are frosted pills at `14px` radius with a color dot. Primary CTA is a full-pill.
- Icons: Lucide (substituted) at 1.75px round stroke.
